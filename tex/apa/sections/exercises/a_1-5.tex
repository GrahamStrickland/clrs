% Exercise A.1-5

We have for $\lvert x \rvert < 1$,
\begin{equation*}
  \begin{split}
    \frac{d}{dx} \sum_{k = 1}^\infty (2k + 1)
    &= \sum_{k = 1}^\infty (2k + 1)x^{2k} \\
    &= \frac{d}{dx} \frac{1}{1 - x^{(2k + 1) - k}} \\
    &= \frac{d}{dx} \frac{1}{1 - x^{k + 1}} \\
    &= \frac{(k + 1)x^k}{{(1 - x^{k + 1})}^2}.
  \end{split}
\end{equation*}
